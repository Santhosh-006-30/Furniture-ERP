# Customer Portal User Manual
**Shiv Furniture Works E-Commerce Portal Guide**

Welcome to the Shiv Furniture Works online portal! This guide explains how to use the shopping tools, manage payments, and track orders.

---

## 1. Registering & Managing Profiles
1. Navigate to `/customer/register` on your browser.
2. Fill in your name, active email, password, shipping address, and optional business details (GSTIN/Company name).
3. Once registered, log in at `/customer/login`. You can update your profile parameters at `/customer/profile` at any time.

---

## 2. Browsing & Product Tools
*   **Wishlist**: Tap the heart icon on any product page to save it. View your saved collection at `/customer/wishlist` where you can move items directly to the cart.
*   **Product Comparison**: Select up to 4 items on the catalog and click "Compare" to view dimensions, wood materials, warranty terms, and pricing side-by-side.
*   **Customer Reviews**: You can read ratings submitted by verified buyers or add your own review once your order has been marked as fully delivered.

---

## 3. Cart & Checkout Processes
1. Add items to your Cart and proceed to `/customer/checkout`.
2. **Coupons**: Type a valid promotion code (e.g. `WELCOME10`) into the promo input field to receive a percentage or flat discount.
3. **Loyalty Points**: Check the "Redeem Points" box to spend accrued loyalty balances (1 point = ₹1 discount).
4. **Payment Processing**:
    *   Choose **Pay Online** to process card or UPI transactions via Razorpay.
    *   Choose **Cash on Delivery** to pay upon receipt of goods.

---

## 4. Tracking and Support
*   Go to `/customer/orders` to view transaction history and tracking status.
*   Click **Track Shipment** to view shipment timelines and delivery steps.
*   Download PDF invoices for completed purchases under `/customer/invoices`.
*   **Returns**: If you receive a damaged or incorrect piece of furniture, go to `/customer/returns` and submit a return request. You can track pick-up status and refund updates in real-time.
